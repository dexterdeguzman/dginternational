Privacy Policy

De Guzman International Visa Consultancy is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information obtained through our contact form.

When you use our contact form, we may collect the following personal information:

  1. Full Name

  3. Phone Number
  4. Nationality
  5. Type of Visa Inquiry(optional)
  6. Message Content

2. Purpose of Collection

We collect this information solely for the following purposes:

  1. To respond to your inquiry

All information submitted through our contact form is sent directly to our secured email inbox. We implement reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the personal data we receive from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may disclose your data only if required to do so by law or in response to a legal request or government inquiry.

Under applicable data protection laws (such as the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012), you have the right to:

  1. Access the personal data we hold about you
  2. Request correction or deletion of your data
  3. Withdraw your consent (if applicable)
  4. Lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [your email address].

6. Retention of Data

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by applicable laws and regulations.
