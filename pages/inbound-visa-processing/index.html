Documentation

What You'll Need

Prepare these documents to ensure a smooth and efficient visa processing experience.

Pre-Arrival Documents

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Visa approval letter or entry permit
  • Round-trip or onward flight booking
  • Proof of accommodation

Post-Arrival Requirements

  • Bureau of Immigration registration
  • ACR I-Card application (stays over 59 days)
  • Visa conversion documents (if applicable)
  • Medical clearance (for certain visa types)
