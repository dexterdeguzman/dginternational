Our Services

Comprehensive Citizenship Solutions

From Balikbayan privileges to full dual citizenship, we provide expert guidance every step of the way.

Balikbayan Privilege

Former Filipino citizens and their families can enjoy visa-free entry for up to one year. We verify eligibility and assist with documentation for hassle-free travel.

Dual Citizenship (RA 9225)

Republic Act 9225 allows natural-born Filipinos who became foreign citizens to re-acquire Filipino citizenship while retaining their foreign nationality.

Re-acquisition of Citizenship

Complete assistance with the oath-taking process, petition filing, and documentation for re-acquiring Filipino citizenship through the proper legal channels.

What You Need

Required Documents

Prepare these documents before your consultation. We'll guide you through every detail.

For Balikbayan Privilege

  • Valid foreign passport
  • Proof of former Filipino citizenship (old Philippine passport, birth certificate)
  • Proof of relationship to Filipino citizen (if applicable)
  • Completed arrival/departure card

For Dual Citizenship (RA 9225)

  • Philippine birth certificate (PSA copy)
  • Foreign naturalization certificate
  • Valid foreign passport
  • Passport-size photos (white background)
  • Oath of allegiance affidavit
